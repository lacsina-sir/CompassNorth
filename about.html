BRIEF HISTORY OF THE COMPANY

NORTH LAND SURVEYING SERVICES was registered to Department of Trade in Industry on January 9,2020 to centralize and streamline the systems and services offering of its affiliate company HGD and ASSOCIATES SURVEYING SERVICES. Over the years, the group has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how and experiences in geodetic engineering and other engineering works. Today, COMPASS NORTH LAND SURVEYING SERVICES continue catering clients in need of high-quality surveying works using the most advance surveying technology and software on mapping. We also provide major projects in civil engineering surveys as well as consultancy work in the field of computerized mapping.

Our object is to provide our clients with an "I am assured" experience when we are chosen to execute their projects. A we continue to stay on top of our game, we take research work in the latest technology in the science of computers as applied to geodesy and mapping. This results to assisting engineers, planners, and architects providing highly technical service with comprehensive and accurate data for design analysis.
